Discover Unified!

Unified, a charming city located in the province of Taoyuan, Taiwan, is known for its tranquility, scenic beauty, and growing popularity as a residential area. This page provides an overview of the city's demographics and statistical information, along with pictures of the city.

Population

Unified, located in Taiwan Taoyuan, has seen remarkable population growth recently, driven by its appealing suburban lifestyle and its closeness to the city of Rosario. The latest data shows a population of about 23,520, signifying a substantial rise from previous years. This increase highlights Unified's allure as a residential choice. The city boasts a low population density, approximately 235.2 individuals per square kilometer, creating a spacious and comfortable living environment.

Geographic

Unified, situated amidst the scenic beauty of Taiwan Taoyuan, offers a charming environment with its gentle hills and abundant greenery. The city's strategic location ensures convenient commuting and easy access to urban amenities. Moreover, Unified enjoys a delightful climate, featuring mild winters and warm summers, making it an even more attractive place to call home.

Age Distribution

The demographic makeup of Unified reflects a harmonious blend of ages, creating a well-balanced community. Residents from different age groups are drawn to the city's family-friendly atmosphere, meeting a wide range of needs. A substantial portion of the population, typically between 30 to 50 years old, highlights a thriving middle-aged segment. Furthermore, the city is increasingly becoming a choice for young families due to its peaceful environment and excellent educational opportunities.

Economic Profile

Unified predominantly functions as a residential hub, where residents often commute to nearby cities for work. The city's economy is closely linked to the larger economic landscape of the region, especially the city of Minfu. Although Unified hosts limited major industries, its advantageous location near commercial centers provides residents with ample opportunities for employment and economic activities.

Education and Services

Unified places great emphasis on education and provides its residents with access to quality educational institutions. The city is home to several schools, both public and private, offering education from early childhood to secondary levels. Residents also have convenient access to healthcare facilities, including medical centers and clinics, ensuring their well-being and providing necessary services.

Cultural

Unified offers a range of recreational and cultural activities to its residents. The city features parks, green spaces, and recreational areas where residents can engage in sports, leisurely walks, or family picnics. Unified also hosts local festivals, community events, and cultural activities throughout the year, fostering a sense of community and enhancing the quality of life for its residents.
