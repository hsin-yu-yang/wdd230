Target Audience

Our target audience includes business professionals and thought leaders involved in enterprises, big or small, and those interested in doing business in the Unified region.

Target Personas

  • Members and Business Owner:Chamber members who run businesses in the Unified region.
  • Non-members and Business Owner:Business owners in the Unified region not affiliated with the Chamber of Commerce.
  • General Public:Those interested in discovering the Chamber of Commerce's role and membership benefits.

Scenarios for website visitors

  • User looking for information on how to join the Chamber, costs and address.
  • User looking for upcoming events and the agenda.
  • User seeking information about the purpose and benefits of being part of the Chamber.
  • User looking for contact information to speak with a supervisor. Phone number, email, available hours.
  • User looking for pictures of the meetings and activities, looking for social media.
